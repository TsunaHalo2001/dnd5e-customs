# Havel The Rock

## Difficulty

* Easy: $1 - 8$
* Medium: $9 - 13$
* Hard: $14 - 20$

## Traits

***Fear immunity***. Havel cannot be frightened.

***Magic resistance***. Havel has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
When equipped with ***Dragon Tooth*** in both hands, Havel has immunity to magic damage.

***Fear aura***. Any creature that starts its turn within $10$ feet of Havel must succeed on a DC $17$ Wisdom saving throw or will have disadvantage on the first attack to Havel that turn.

***Imperturbable step***. Havel can move across difficult terrain without expending extra movement, cannot be knocked, prone except by effects that ignore those immunitities.

***Night vision***. Havel can fully see in darkness, both magical and nonmagical, to a distance of $120$ feet.

***Invisible perception***. Havel can see invisible creatures and objects within $30$ feet of it.

***Immune to disarm***. Havel cannot be disarmed of its weapon, shield or armor.

## Actions

***Attack: Dragon Tooth.*** *Melee Attack Roll*: $+10$ to hit, reach $10$ ft., targets straight line in range. *Hit*: $10(2d6 + 4)$, $14(2d8 + 6)$ or $18(2d10 + 8)$ bludgeoning damage. *Effect*: The target must succeed on a DC $15$, $17$ or $19$ Dexterity saving throw or be pushed $10$ feet away from Havel and if the target is small or medium and the DC throw fails for $5$ or more be knocked prone.

***Special Attack: Rock Slam.*** *Special Melee Attack*: (No attack roll, automatic hit) reach $10$ ft., targets straight line in range. *Recoil*: Havel cannot move or react the round after using this action. *Hit*: $14(2d10 + 4)$, $21(3d10 + 6)$ or $28(4d10 + 8)$ bludgeoning damage. *Effect*: The target must succeed on a DC $15$, $17$ or $19$ Dexterity saving throw to reduce damage in half or be pushed $10$ feet away from Havel and if the target is small or medium and the DC throw fails for $5$ or more be knocked prone.

## Reactions

***Shield block.*** When Havel is hit by an attack, it can use its reaction to reduce the damage by $5(1d10)$, $8(1d12)$ or $11(2d10)$, if the attack is magical damage the reduction is an additional $3(1d6)$, $4(1d8)$ or $8(2d8)$.
*Effect*: If the damage is reduced to $0$. The attacker must succeed on a DC $15$, $17$ or $19$ Strength saving throw or be pushed $5$ feet away from Havel.

## Item Description

***Havel's Ring***. While wearing this ring, the wearer’s maximum carrying capacity, allows to use Havel's equipment without any strength requirement.

***Dragon Tooth***. *Type:* Martial, bludgeon, magical. *Requirements*: Strength $18$ or higher to maintain magical properties. *Properties*: Heavy, One-Handed, Two-Handed. *Description*: A massive greatclub fashioned from the tooth of a dragon. While wielding this weapon, the wielder gains a $+5$ bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. *Hit:* One-Handed $8(1d10 + 3)$ bludgeoning damage. *Two-Handed* $9(2d8 + 3)$ bludgeoning damage. *Special property*: When using this weapon in both hands, the wielder gains advantage on saving throws against spells.

***Havel's Shield***. *Type:* Shield, magical. *Requirements*: Strength $16$ or higher to maintain benefical properties. *Properties*: $+3$ AC bonus. *Description*: A large, sturdy shield that provides excellent protection. *Special property*: The wielder gains resistance against bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from non-magical attacks.

***Havel's Armor***. *Type:* Heavy Armor, magical. *Requirements*: Strength $18$ or higher to maintain benefical properties. *Properties*: AC $18$ + Dex modifier (max $1$). *Description*: A set of heavy armor that offers superior protection. *Special property*: The wearer gains resistance to all spell damage.
